About GrapesGenix

Get Trained by the Experts Who Do It Best

Founded in 2015, GrapesGenix Technical Solutions Pvt Ltd has emerged as a premier technology solution provider and IT training institute in Kerala. We specialize in enterprise software development, mobile application builds, web engineering, Industry on Campus initiatives, and academic training courses.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic theory and active industrial engineering. Through our hands-on mentorship, students build production-level software under the guidance of active developers, making them highly competitive in the modern recruitment market.

Our Vision

To cultivate the next generation of engineers, empowering them with the skills to turn innovative ideas into active technologies.
